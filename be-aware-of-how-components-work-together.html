Be aware of how your components work together

8 July 2019

The NHS UK components from the service manual are very useful. But be aware of how your components work together, not just in isolation.

For example, the inset text component. Use inset to help users identify important content on the page.

Inset text is really useful for making content stand out amongst other content.

However, imagine you use a warning callout close to some inset text. How will users know what content is important?

Although the inset text and warning callout components have different purposes, on the whole they still indicate to a user that this content is important.

Using too many of these components close together reduces their effectiveness and could confuse your users.
